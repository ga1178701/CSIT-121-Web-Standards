Places I Have Visited in the US

Published on September 14, 2025 by Prabhakar Gautam

St. Louis Arch, Missouri

The Gateway Arch in St. Louis is an incredible monument that symbolizes the expansion to the West. Standing beneath it and riding to the top gave me breathtaking views of the Mississippi River and the city.

What I Liked

  1. The futuristic tram ride to the top
  2. Amazing view of the city and river
  3. Learning the history of westward expansion

Learn more here: Gateway Arch Official Site

New York City

Visiting New York City was like stepping into a movie. The energy, the skyscrapers, and the diversity of people made it an unforgettable experience.

What I Liked

  • Walking through Times Square at night
  • Peaceful moments in Central Park

Learn more here: NYC Official Guide

Washington, DC

Washington, DC is filled with history and monuments. From the White House to the National Mall, I was amazed by the significance of every landmark.

What I Liked
